G5R Monitor Trace Tips Info
  • To Zoom, Left Click on a chart and move right or left
  • To Pan, Ctrl+ Left Click on a chart and move right or left
  • To Reset Zoom/Pan., Left Click on a Reset zoom buton on the chart
  • To Add a Marker, Shift + Left Click on a chart. Available only in line charts
  • To remove one marker, Click on the x on marker in the 'Marker List' table
  • To remove all markers, Click on the Remove All Marker in the top
  • To highlight a particlur series, Hover on the series name in the legend
  • To hide/show a particlur series, Click on the series name in the legend
